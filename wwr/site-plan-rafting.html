Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this page is to show and offer our services to potential customers, by boosting them with client stories, photos and videos of their exeperiences with us on our boats.

Audience

Our audience is defined by these scenarios and questions: Who are the target customers? Out target customers are people older than 18 years, willing to spent a rationable part of their vacation budget in adventure sports; What do they expect to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They look for all the location information, client opinions and experiences, and safety indications and rating from real customers; How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? They can access the website from any device, but mostly on mobile.
